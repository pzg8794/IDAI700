\begin{frame}{Case Study: Ransomware at Dialysis Clinic}
\textbf{Summary:} In 2023, a regional dialysis center was targeted by a ransomware attack. Critical patient data was encrypted, leading to missed appointments, delayed diagnoses, and systemic disruptions. The AI system used to schedule treatments and flag urgent results became inaccessible. Multiple patients experienced severe medical setbacks.

\pause
\textbf{Equity Impact:} The most affected patients were low-income individuals relying on public transit and non-English-speaking families unfamiliar with digital rescheduling systems.

\pause
\textbf{Prompt:} What could have been done differently at the network and AI system level to prevent this harm? How would YOU prioritize protections?
\end{frame}
