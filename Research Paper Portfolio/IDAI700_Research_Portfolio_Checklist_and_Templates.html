IDAI-700 Research Portfolio — Checklist + 6 Entry Templates

Theme: How can we design equitable AI systems that healthcare providers and patients from marginalized (CLD) communities can trust?

Submission Checklist

Global Pre‑Flight (do these first)

  • All 6 sources are peer‑reviewed AND contain substantive ethical arguments (not “how‑to” or news).
  • Searched via RIT Library and checked the “Scholarly & Peer Reviewed” filter.
  • Chose one citation style (APA/MLA/Chicago) and will use it consistently across all entries.
  • Noted late policy (−5 points/day).

What to write for EACH article (~800 words total)

  • Two parts: (1) Summary (~350–450 words), (2) Evaluation (~350–450 words).
  • You may use first‑person (“I”) in the Evaluation section.
  • Track word subtotals for Summary and Evaluation and a combined total.

Mini Rubric (keep this under each entry)

20% Source quality & relevance    40% Summary quality (argument / evidence / reasoning)    40% Evaluation quality (strengths / weaknesses / persuasiveness)

Submission & Follow‑ups

  • Use consistent formatting (one citation style, clean layout).
  • Submit to MyCourses by the deadline.
  • After portfolio: Nov 14 post research question; Nov 17 help a classmate; Nov 19 post one entry; Nov 24 provide ~400‑word feedback.
